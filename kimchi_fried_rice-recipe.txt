Ingredients:
 - 1 cup Kimchi (including juice)
 - 1 bowl rice
 - 1 egg
 - 2 tbs soy sauce
 - 4 tbs oil

Directions:
  1. Make sunny-side-up fried egg.
  2. Add oil and stir-fry kimchi.
  3. Add rice and mix well with kimchi.
  4. Add soy sauce and stir well.
  5. Plate fried rice and put fried egg on it.
